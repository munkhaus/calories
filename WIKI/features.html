✨ App Features

This page details the app's functionalities.

Activity Tracking

This feature allows users to log their physical activities, such as running, cycling, or gym workouts. The application then calculates the estimated calorie expenditure based on the activity type, duration, and user's profile (e.g., weight, age) to help them track their overall energy balance.

Dashboard

The Dashboard provides users with a comprehensive overview of their daily progress and key metrics. It typically displays total calories consumed and burned, macronutrient breakdown, remaining calories for the day, and quick access to logging meals or activities.

Food Database

This feature provides access to an extensive database of food items, including packaged foods, fresh ingredients, and restaurant meals, complete with detailed nutritional information (calories, proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, minerals). Users can search this database when logging their meals.

Food Logging

Food Logging is a core feature enabling users to record the food and beverages they consume. Users can search the food database, scan barcodes, or manually enter items to track their caloric and nutritional intake accurately throughout the day.

Info

The Info section provides users with helpful information, tips, and educational content related to nutrition, healthy eating, exercise, and using the app effectively. It may include articles, FAQs, and links to relevant resources.

Logging

This refers to the general capability of the app to record various types of data. While "Food Logging" and "Activity Tracking" are specific types of logging, this could also encompass logging water intake, mood, or other user-defined metrics relevant to their health journey.

Onboarding

The Onboarding process guides new users through the initial setup of the application. This typically includes creating an account, providing personal details (age, weight, height, activity level), setting health goals (e.g., weight loss, maintenance), and learning about the app's main features.

Planning

The Planning feature allows users to plan their meals and activities in advance. This can help with meal prepping, ensuring nutritional goals are met, and maintaining consistency with their health and fitness regimen. Users might be able to create meal plans or schedule workouts.

Profile

The Profile section allows users to manage their personal information, settings, and preferences. This includes details like age, weight, height, dietary restrictions, activity level, and app-specific settings such as notification preferences or connected accounts.

Progress

This feature enables users to track their progress over time towards their health and fitness goals. It usually involves charts, graphs, and summaries of data such as weight changes, calorie intake trends, activity levels, and nutrient consumption patterns.

Splash

The Splash screen is the first screen users see when they open the application. It typically displays the app's logo or branding for a brief period while the app loads necessary resources in the background before transitioning to the main interface or onboarding flow.

Weight Tracking

Weight Tracking allows users to regularly log their body weight. The app then visualizes this data, often in a graph, to help users monitor changes, track progress towards weight-related goals (loss, gain, or maintenance), and understand long-term trends.
